\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

An explicit affine permutation converts each finite-window Zeckendorf fiber
multiplicity into a Fibonacci-lag difference of the indexed partition function.
This identification transfers the full pressure sequence $(P_q)_{q\ge 0}$ to
the collision moments, realizes each partition constant $\lambda_q$ ($q\ge 2$)
as the Perron root of a polynomial-size nonnegative integer matrix, and
assembles a closed canonical/microcanonical formalism: the adjacent pressure
slopes $\Delta_q$ select the canonical thickness bands, the zero-temperature
limit recovers $D_m^{1/m}\to\sqrt{\varphi}$, and the R\'enyi entropy-rate
spectrum of the visible law is the $q=1$ projection of the same pressure
geometry.

In the arithmetic window $q=9,\dots,17$, the recurrence polynomials of
$S_q(m)$ are irreducible with full symmetric Galois groups.  For the
four-element block $q\in\{12,13,14,15\}$, squareclass independence of
discriminants certifies linear disjointness of the splitting fields and
yields product Chebotarev densities.  Extension of these arithmetic
certificates beyond $q=17$ and symbolic root-isolation for the secondary
spectral pattern remain open.
