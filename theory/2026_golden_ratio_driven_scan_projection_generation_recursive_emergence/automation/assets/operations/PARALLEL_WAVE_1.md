4. E2 Traceability:

5. E2 Rewrite:

6. F Traceability + theorem inventory:

7. F Rewrite:

8. G/H Bibliography and recon:
   target-journal paper reading, bibliography scope, journal-fit notes.
9. Lean Sync:
   cross-check labels, declarations, and backlog across all active tracks.
10. Integrator:
   merges outputs, updates statuses, prepares the next round.

## Why this split

- Traceability is the hard prerequisite repeatedly skipped in manual runs.
- Rewrite work should not start before the paper is auditable.
- Lean sync should run in parallel instead of blocking prose work.
