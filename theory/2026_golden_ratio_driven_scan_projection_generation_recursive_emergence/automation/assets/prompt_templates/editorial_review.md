# Prompt 03: Editorial Review

Assume the role of a handling editor or serious referee for `{{JOURNAL}}`.

Read the manuscript as it currently stands and answer the following:

1. Would you accept, reject, or request major revision?
2. What are the main mathematical or editorial blockers?
3. Which parts still sound synthetic, inflated, repetitive, or structurally weak?
4. Which definitions, theorem statements, proof transitions, bibliography choices, or appendices should be cut, merged, or rewritten?

Do not give generic encouragement. Give a decision-grade review.
