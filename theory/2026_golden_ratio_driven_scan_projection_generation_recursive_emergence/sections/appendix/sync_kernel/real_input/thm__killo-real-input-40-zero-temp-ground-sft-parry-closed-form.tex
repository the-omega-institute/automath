\begin{theorem}[零温四态地基 SFT 的 Perron 向量与 Parry 测度闭式]\label{thm:killo-real-input-40-zero-temp-ground-sft-parry-closed-form}
\leanverified{paper\_killo\_real\_input\_40\_zero\_temp\_ground\_sft\_parry\_closed\_form}
沿用命题 \ref{prop:real-input-40-pressure-freezing} 之后零温缩放极限的记号，记
\[
\rho:=\rho(B_\infty).
\]
则可显式取右 Perron 特征向量
\[
v=
\begin{pmatrix}
(\rho-3)^2\\[1mm]
\rho^{-1}\\[1mm]
1\\[1mm]
\rho-3
\end{pmatrix},
\qquad
B_\infty v=\rho v,
\]
以及左 Perron 特征向量
\[
\ell=
\begin{pmatrix}
\rho^{-1}\\[1mm]
\rho^{-2}\\[1mm]
\rho-3\\[1mm]
1
\end{pmatrix},
\qquad
\ell^{\top}B_\infty=\rho\,\ell^{\top}.
\]
由此，Parry 极大熵转移矩阵
\[
P_{ij}=\frac{(B_\infty)_{ij}v_j}{\rho v_i}
\]
化为完全闭式
\[
P=
\begin{pmatrix}
0 & \dfrac{1}{1+\rho} & \dfrac{\rho}{1+\rho} & 0\\[3mm]
0 & 0 & 1 & 0\\[3mm]
0 & 0 & \dfrac{3}{\rho} & 1-\dfrac{3}{\rho}\\[3mm]
1-\dfrac{3}{\rho} & 0 & 0 & \dfrac{3}{\rho}
\end{pmatrix}.
\]
相应平稳分布 \(\pi_i\propto \ell_i v_i\) 满足
\[
\pi
=
\frac{1}{Z}
\left(
\frac{\rho+1}{\rho^3},
\frac{1}{\rho^3},
\rho-3,
\rho-3
\right),
\qquad
Z=\frac{\rho+2}{\rho^3}+2(\rho-3).
\]
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
由零温极限的特征多项式
\[
\det(I-wB_\infty)=1-6w+9w^2-w^3-w^4
\]
可知 \(\rho\) 满足
\[
\rho^4-6\rho^3+9\rho^2-\rho-1=0.
\]
将其分别除以 \(\rho\) 与 \(\rho^2\)，得到两条恒等式
\[
\rho^2(\rho-3)^2=\rho+1,
\qquad
\rho^{-1}+\rho^{-2}=(\rho-3)^2.
\]

先证右 Perron 向量。直接计算
\[
B_\infty v
=
\begin{pmatrix}
\rho^{-1}+1\\
1\\
3+(\rho-3)\\
(\rho-3)^2+3(\rho-3)
\end{pmatrix}.
\]
其中第一分量由 \(\rho^2(\rho-3)^2=\rho+1\) 化为
\[
\rho^{-1}+1=\rho(\rho-3)^2,
\]
而第四分量满足
\[
(\rho-3)^2+3(\rho-3)=\rho(\rho-3).
\]
故 \(B_\infty v=\rho v\)。

再证左 Perron 向量。直接计算
\[
\ell^{\top}B_\infty
=
\bigl(1,\ \rho^{-1},\ \rho^{-1}+\rho^{-2}+3(\rho-3),\ \rho\bigr).
\]
由上式 \(\rho^{-1}+\rho^{-2}=(\rho-3)^2\)，第三分量化简为
\[
(\rho-3)^2+3(\rho-3)=\rho(\rho-3),
\]
故 \(\ell^{\top}B_\infty=\rho\,\ell^{\top}\)。

再算 Parry 转移矩阵。由定义，
\[
P_{12}
=
\frac{1\cdot v_2}{\rho v_1}
=
\frac{1/\rho}{\rho(\rho-3)^2}
=
\frac{1}{\rho^2(\rho-3)^2}
=
\frac{1}{1+\rho},
\]
\[
P_{13}
=
\frac{1\cdot v_3}{\rho v_1}
=
\frac{1}{\rho(\rho-3)^2}
=
\frac{\rho}{1+\rho}.
\]
其余非零项直接化简为
\[
P_{23}=1,\qquad
P_{33}=\frac{3}{\rho},\qquad
P_{34}=\frac{\rho-3}{\rho}=1-\frac{3}{\rho},
\]
\[
P_{41}=\frac{(\rho-3)^2}{\rho(\rho-3)}=\frac{\rho-3}{\rho}=1-\frac{3}{\rho},
\qquad
P_{44}=\frac{3(\rho-3)}{\rho(\rho-3)}=\frac{3}{\rho}.
\]
于是得到所示 \(P\)。

最后，由 Parry 公式 \(\pi_i\propto \ell_i v_i\)，并逐项计算
\[
\ell_1v_1=\frac{(\rho-3)^2}{\rho}=\frac{\rho+1}{\rho^3},
\qquad
\ell_2v_2=\frac{1}{\rho^3},
\]
\[
\ell_3v_3=\rho-3,
\qquad
\ell_4v_4=\rho-3.
\]
再按
\[
Z=\frac{\rho+2}{\rho^3}+2(\rho-3)
\]
归一化，即得所示平稳分布闭式。证毕。
\end{proof}
