\subsubsection{真实输入下的三维 arity--负携带--输出素数谱（3x3x3）}\label{app:real-input-40-arity-prime-spectrum-3d}
在二维 $(\chi,N_-)$ 之外，再引入输出计数
\[
\eta:=e,\qquad N_e(\gamma):=\sum_{k=1}^{|\gamma|}\eta_k.
\]
定义三维生成函数
\[
P_n(q,r,u):=\sum_{\substack{\gamma\ \mathrm{primitive}\|\gamma|=n}} q^{\chi(\gamma)}r^{N_-(\gamma)}u^{N_e(\gamma)}.
\]
对模 $(3,3,3)$ 的 Dirichlet 分层，记
\[
p_{n,(a,b,c)}^{(3,3,3)}
=\#\{\gamma:\ |\gamma|=n,\ \chi(\gamma)\equiv a,\ N_-(\gamma)\equiv b,\ N_e(\gamma)\equiv c\ (\mathrm{mod}\ 3)\},
\]
则有
\[
\sum_{n\le N}\frac{p_{n,(a,b,c)}^{(3,3,3)}}{\lambda^n}
=\frac{1}{27}\log N+C_{a,b,c}^{(3,3,3)}+o(1).
\]
将 $c$ 作为切片指标（按 $N_e\bmod 3$ 分三张 $3\times 3$ 矩阵），数值为
\[
C^{(3,3,3)}\big|_{c=0}\approx
\begin{pmatrix}
0.514571241281 & -0.114943129154 & -0.115059813670\\
-0.081106021129 & -0.086503659089 & -0.089908304252\\
-0.101600045183 & -0.014463141143 & -0.030060035952
\end{pmatrix},
\]
\[
C^{(3,3,3)}\big|_{c=1}\approx
\begin{pmatrix}
-0.101678142150 & -0.046372925882 & \ \ 0.107341140580\\
\ \ 0.924428415504 & \ \ 0.154852746931 & -0.113496112402\\
-0.084657471942 & -0.085378903632 & -0.096146968376
\end{pmatrix},
\]
\[
C^{(3,3,3)}\big|_{c=2}\approx
\begin{pmatrix}
-0.090541121520 & -0.077618901650 & -0.089302648708\\
-0.058681839494 & \ \ 0.004038838860 & -0.029353054870\\
\ \ 0.312181425326 & -0.116041151361 & -0.071597413922
\end{pmatrix},
\]
并满足 $\sum_{a,b,c}C_{a,b,c}^{(3,3,3)}=\mathsf{M}_{\varphi^2}$。
最坏扭曲谱半径为
\[
\rho_{3,3,3}\approx 2.4522766755,\qquad \rho_{3,3,3}/\lambda\approx 0.9366863402,
\]
从而
\[
p_{n,(a,b,c)}^{(3,3,3)}=\frac{p_n}{27}+O\!\left(\frac{\rho_{3,3,3}^n}{n}\right).
\]
