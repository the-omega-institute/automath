在同一椭圆曲线模型
\[
E:\ Y^{2}=X^{3}-X+\frac14,
\qquad
y:=X^{2}+Y-\frac12
\]
及其诱导的谱四次平面曲线
\[
\Pi(X,y):=X^{4}-X^{3}-(2y+1)X^{2}+X+y(y+1)=0

\[
N_{6}(X):=2X^{6}-10X^{4}+10X^{3}-10X^{2}+2X+1

T_{4}(y):=\operatorname{Res}_{X}\bigl(N_{6}(X),\Pi(X,y)\bigr)\in\ZZ[y].

\[
