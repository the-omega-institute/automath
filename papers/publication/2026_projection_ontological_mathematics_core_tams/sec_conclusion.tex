\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

An explicit affine permutation converts each finite-window Zeckendorf fiber
multiplicity into a Fibonacci-lag difference of the indexed partition function.
This identification transfers the full pressure sequence $(p_q)_{q\ge 0}$ to
the collision moments, realizes each partition constant $\lambda_q$ ($q\ge 2$)
as the Perron root of a polynomial-size nonnegative integer matrix, and
assembles a closed canonical/microcanonical formalism: the adjacent pressure
slopes $\Delta_q$ select the canonical thickness bands, the zero-temperature
limit recovers $D_m^{1/m}\to\sqrt{\varphi}$, and the integer-order R\'enyi
entropy rates of the visible law are the $q=1$ projection of the same pressure
geometry.

Section~\ref{sec:chebotarev} records a finite audited arithmetic window
$q=9,\dots,17$, in which the recurrence polynomials of $S_q(m)$ are
computationally certified to be irreducible with full symmetric Galois groups.
For the four-element block $q\in\{12,13,14,15\}$, the same audit indicates
linear disjointness of the splitting fields and the resulting product
Chebotarev density.  Extension of these arithmetic certificates beyond
$q=17$ and symbolic root-isolation for the secondary spectral pattern remain
open.
