\section{Visible words}\label{sec:spg}

An event \(\mathsf P\in\mathcal F_m\) is determined entirely by its
length-\(m\) observed words: it is a union of cylinders \(C_a\) with
\(a\in\mathcal A_{\mathrm{obs}}^m\).  On
\(\mathcal A_{\mathrm{obs}}^{\NNzero}\) we use the prefix ultrametric
\[
  d_{\mathrm{pre}}(\xi,\eta):=2^{-\ell(\xi,\eta)},
\]
where \(\ell(\xi,\eta)\) is the first index at which \(\xi\) and
\(\eta\) differ.  The box-counting notation in
Section~\ref{sec:clarity} refers to this metric.
