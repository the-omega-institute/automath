\section{Visible words}\label{sec:spg}

An event $\mathsf{P}\in\mathcal{F}_m$ is determined entirely by its
length-$m$ address prefixes: $\mathsf{P}$ corresponds to a union of
cylinders $[a]$, $a\in A\subseteq\Sigma^m$, and each such cylinder is
clopen in the prefix ultrametric
$d_{\mathrm{pre}}(\xi,\eta):=2^{-\ell(\xi,\eta)}$.
