### Reviewer Rotation Protocol

1. **Codex general review** (first pass, cheap)
2. **Codex self-fix** (fix its own findings)
3. **ChatGPT review** (via oracle bridge)
4. **Codex fix** (from ChatGPT feedback)
5. **Claude review** (deep mathematical verification)
6. **Claude/Codex fix** (remaining issues)
7. **ChatGPT final validation** (acceptance gate)

Minimum 5 rounds per paper before marking ready.
