\section{Extended examples and tables}
\label{app:extended-examples}

Extended examples, large tables, and exhaustive enumerations should live in the supplement or audit repository rather than in the main paper.  Suitable items include:
\begin{itemize}
\item complete folded histogram tables for selected depths;
\item examples where naive truncation fails to commute with folding;
\item small finite graph presentations and their entropy statistics;
\item boundary-cylinder counts for representative events;
\item negative or null examples where sample size is insufficient.
\end{itemize}
