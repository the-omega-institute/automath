\section{Scan error profile proofs}
\label{app:clarity-proofs}

The scan error profile is an ordinary finite Bayes decision problem on the cylinder partition \(\mathcal C_m\).  On each cylinder \(C\), every \(\mathcal F_m\)-measurable binary classifier is constant.  The optimal choice is \(1\) when \(\mu(P\cap C)\ge \mu(C\setminus P)\) and \(0\) otherwise.  The cylinder contribution to the error is therefore
\[
\min\{\mu(P\cap C),\mu(C\setminus P)\}.
\]
Summing over all cylinders gives \Cref{prop:cylinder-decomposition}.  The boundary-dimension estimate follows by observing that non-boundary cylinders contribute zero and boundary cylinders contribute at most \(\mu(C)/2\).
