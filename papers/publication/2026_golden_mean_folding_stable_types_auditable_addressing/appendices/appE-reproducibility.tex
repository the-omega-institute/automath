\section{Reproducibility protocol}
\label{app:reproducibility}

The reproducibility package should contain:
\[
(\text{code},\text{data},\text{configuration},\text{seed},\text{environment},\text{output hash}).
\]
For every finite-sample statistical claim, the package should record \(m\), \(N\), \(N_{\mathrm{eff}}\), the distance or divergence used, the uncertainty radius, the model tolerance, and the decision rule.  For dependent or overlapping windows, the effective sample size must be stated explicitly.
