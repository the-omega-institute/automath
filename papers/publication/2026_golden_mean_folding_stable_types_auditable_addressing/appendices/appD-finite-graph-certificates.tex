\section{Finite graph and entropy certificates}
\label{app:finite-graph-certificates}

A finite graph certificate for \(X_m\) should specify:
\begin{itemize}
\item the state set and transition relation;
\item the accepted stable language at depth \(m\);
\item the adjacency matrix \(A_m\);
\item the Perron--Frobenius eigenvalue when used;
\item the entropy or pressure statistic being reported;
\item the baseline distribution, such as the Parry measure, and its support.
\end{itemize}
These objects are certificate interfaces.  They support auditing of stable type generation but do not identify the data-generating mechanism by themselves.
