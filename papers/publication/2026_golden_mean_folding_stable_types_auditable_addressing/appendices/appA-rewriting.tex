\section{Rewriting system: termination and confluence}
\label{app:rewriting}

This appendix records the proof obligations for the golden carry-elimination system used in \Cref{thm:golden-fold-normal-form}.  A full formal audit file should verify:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(A\arabic*)]
\item every rewrite rule preserves Fibonacci value;
\item the boundary convention for carries leaving the visible window is deterministic;
\item rewriting terminates by a well-founded measure on illegal adjacent pairs and carry height;
\item all critical overlaps of adjacent carry rules are locally confluent;
\item Newman's lemma applies, yielding global confluence;
\item the unique normal word is the Zeckendorf prefix of \(N_m(\omega)\).
\end{enumerate}

The main text uses these obligations in theorem form because the first submission is organized around the stable-type generator rather than around a complete catalog of rewrite traces.
