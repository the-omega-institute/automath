\section{Fold-aware restriction and inverse systems}
\label{app:inverse-systems}

The fold-aware restriction data consist of typed maps
\[
\rho^{\Aware}_{n,m}:\Om_n\to\Om_m,
\qquad
r_{n,m}:X_n\to X_m
\qquad(n\ge m),
\]
with
\[
r_{n,m}\Fold_n=\Fold_m\rho^{\Aware}_{n,m}.
\]
The inverse-system audit checks:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(B\arabic*)]
\item \(r_{m,m}=\id\);
\item \(r_{\ell,m}r_{n,\ell}=r_{n,m}\) for \(n\ge\ell\ge m\);
\item the aware restriction is compatible with the finite boundary convention;
\item the naive truncation defect is reported separately and not used as the restriction map.
\end{enumerate}
