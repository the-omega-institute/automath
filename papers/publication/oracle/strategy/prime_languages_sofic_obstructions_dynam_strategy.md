## 3. Recommendation
Prioritize **Option A – Deep mathematical rewrite**. Scope reduction or venue shopping might salvage a publication elsewhere, but they do not address the “too slight” verdict that will reappear whenever reviewers expect a real advance. A deliberate rewrite that delivers new mathematics and a correct Zeckendorf argument has the best chance to win back the current venue and also creates a stronger fallback for other journals.
