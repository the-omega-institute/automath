Major revision

There is a potentially publishable core here, especially in Sections 3 to 6: the fold-aware restriction, the anomaly statistics, the conjugacy threshold, and the order-4 transfer-matrix analysis are interesting, and several central formulas appear internally coherent. But the manuscript is not acceptance-ready. The biggest problems are scope control, proof transparency, and one genuine numerical/spectral error.
