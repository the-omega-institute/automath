\section{Concluding remarks}
\label{sec:discussion}

The finite-window fold isolates a canonical obstruction to naive restriction in Fibonacci numeration. The point of the paper is that this obstruction is governed by a single explicit four-state presentation. The same object controls the bulk discrepancy process, the local inverse for the induced sliding block code, and the weighted fiber formalism. In particular, the exact constants and rational functions obtained in the main text are not unrelated calculations, but different manifestations of one finite-state mechanism.
