\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

This paper is a focused note built around a narrow claim. Every statement is made relative to a chosen gluing-sensitive realization prestack with fixed abelian band; we do not assert that arbitrary set-valued local object functors canonically determine such data, and the scope checklist of \S\ref{subsec:scope-chosen-lift} records exactly when the formalism applies. The logical contribution remains limited to the constant-free equality-only lower reduct evaluated on singleton parameter states of parameter-indexed local-object expansions (\Cref{thm:pointwise-irreducibility}); no broader forcing-necessity statement is claimed. The semantic contribution packages standard gerbe facts relative to the chosen lift: visible branches coincide with the connected components of the realization stack, and gluing failure occurs precisely when compatible local data exist but every component gerbe is non-neutral (\Cref{thm:gerbe-null-semantics}). The homological contribution identifies the presentation-relative visible quotient with the cokernel of the homological evaluation map for a fixed finite nerve presentation, while the blind remainder is the pure $\operatorname{Ext}$-part (\Cref{thm:character-blind-obstructions}). The quotient is well defined only up to the explicit presentation morphisms (equivalences of chosen lifts, changes of band trivialization, and $H_2$-surjective refinements) recorded in \Cref{prop:presentation-groupoid}.

The paper's external point of contact with established work is conditional. In the unique-branch support-presheaf setting, \emph{when a compatible realization prestack exists}, the theory recovers the Abramsky--Brandenburger no-global-section picture and identifies the Car\`u-type cohomological blind cases with the pure $\operatorname{Ext}$-part of the obstruction class (\Cref{thm:unique-branch-contextuality-comparison}). No construction from an arbitrary empirical model to a realization prestack is provided here; such a construction would require groupoid-enriched measurement data beyond the standard Abramsky--Brandenburger formalism. The checklist of \S\ref{subsec:scope-chosen-lift} is verified explicitly by the torus-type construction of \Cref{ex:end-to-end}, while the $S^2$-, $\mathbb{R}P^2$-, and Kripke--Beth examples illustrate the visible and blind extremes. GHZ is retained only as the standard presheaf-level contextuality benchmark of \Cref{rem:ghz-benchmark}.

What has been omitted is deliberate. The measurable recursive-addressing, symbolic-dynamical cylinder, inverse-limit, multi-branch budget, refinement-dynamical, and complexity layers belong to different projects and would require their own separate development. The present paper is intended to stand or fall on the explicitly scoped branch-gerbe semantics of gluing failure and on the identification of the presentation-relative visible quotient and its pure-Ext blind residue.
