\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

The present version is a focused note built around a narrower claim than the longer manuscript. It starts from a chosen local-object layer and a chosen gluing-sensitive realization prestack, rather than from a claim that an arbitrary set-valued local object functor carries a canonical obstruction theory. Its first result is logical: compatible local sectionability and gluing-level absence are not definable in the lower information-state forcing language, so the local-object layer is genuinely necessary (\Cref{thm:pointwise-irreducibility}). Its second result is semantic: visible branches are exactly the connected components of the realization stack, and gluing failure is exactly the situation in which compatible local data exist but every component gerbe is non-neutral (\Cref{thm:gerbe-null-semantics}). Its third result is homological: once a branch obstruction class has been fixed, the visible quotient relative to that chosen presentation is the cokernel of the homological evaluation map, so the visible part of an obstruction is precisely its $H_2$-detected part, while the blind remainder is exactly the pure $\operatorname{Ext}$-residual (\Cref{thm:character-blind-obstructions}). The visible quotient is invariant under equivalence of chosen lifts and common refinement of covers (\Cref{prop:visible-quotient-lift-invariance,prop:visible-quotient-refinement-invariance}).

The paper's external point of contact with established work is conditional. In the unique-branch support-presheaf setting, \emph{when a compatible realization prestack exists}, the theory recovers the Abramsky--Brandenburger no-global-section picture and identifies the Car\`u-type cohomological blind cases with the pure $\operatorname{Ext}$-part of the obstruction class (\Cref{thm:unique-branch-contextuality-comparison}). No construction from an arbitrary empirical model to a realization prestack is provided here; such a construction would require groupoid-enriched measurement data beyond the standard Abramsky--Brandenburger formalism. The worked $S^2$-type and $\mathbb{R}P^2$-type examples isolate the visible and blind extremes, and the end-to-end torus example (\Cref{ex:end-to-end}) validates the full semantic pipeline from reference to visible quotient.

What has been omitted is deliberate. The measurable recursive-addressing, symbolic-dynamical cylinder, inverse-limit, multi-branch budget, refinement-dynamical, and complexity layers belong to a different narrative and would require their own separate development. The present paper is intended to stand or fall on the branch-gerbe semantics of gluing failure and on the identification of the visible quotient and its pure-Ext blind residue.
