Recommendation after revision: accept, subject to normal editorial checks.

Initial weaknesses identified
1. The finite-part section still contained older Dirichlet-Mertens tensor and rotation-polytope material that no longer matched the stated main theorem or the spectral-fingerprint emphasis of the paper.
2. The Chebotarev section mixed core Peter--Weyl equidistribution results with speculative "critical-line comb" claims that were not needed for the ETDS narrative and weakened the paper's focus.
3. The non-abelian class-constant statement was phrased with a boundary holomorphy hypothesis that is too weak for the actual constant formula; the safe formulation requires explicit summability of the non-trivial primitive Peter--Weyl channels at z = lambda^{-1}.
4. The length-congruence Mertens constant had the wrong normalization on the trivial channel: the partial sums carry the Euler-Mascheroni correction, so the constant must involve M(A), not only log Mfrak(A).
5. The introduction still advertised torsion-side results that were no longer present in the body, which created label drift and overclaimed the final draft.
