\section{Open problems}\label{sec:conclusion}

The main theorems identify the exact trace-class threshold for normal
cyclic-block realizability, the resulting normal classification up to zero
summands, and the global trace-norm minimum inside the full determinant
fiber. The remaining gaps are therefore no longer qualitative. They can
be formulated as concrete problems about the residual geometry of the same
determinant fibers.

\begin{enumerate}[label=\textup{(\arabic*)}, leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Determinant fibers modulo trace-class quasinilpotents.}
  For summable cyclic data \(\mathcal D\), write
  \[
    \mathcal F(\mathcal D)
    :=
    \{T\in\sone:\det(I-zT)=D_{\mathcal D}(z)\text{ as a germ at }0\}.
  \]
  By \Cref{cor:nonnormal-fiber-unbounded}, every
  \(L_{\mathcal D}\oplus Q\) with \(Q\in\sone\) quasinilpotent belongs to
  \(\mathcal F(\mathcal D)\). Determine the quotient of
  \(\mathcal F(\mathcal D)\) under the stabilization relation
  \[
    \begin{aligned}
      T\sim_{\mathrm q}T'
      \iff{}& \exists\,Q,Q'\in\sone\text{ quasinilpotent such that} \\
      & T\oplus Q
      \text{ and }
      T'\oplus Q'
      \text{ are unitarily equivalent.}
    \end{aligned}
  \]
  Is every class represented by the canonical model \(L_{\mathcal D}\), or
  do there exist nontrivial stabilized invariants of the nonnormal fiber?

  \item \textbf{Characterize the trace-minimizing fiber.}
  Set
  \[
    \tau_{\mathcal D}
    :=\sum_{j\in J}m_jn_j\abs{\beta_j}^{1/n_j}.
  \]
  \Cref{thm:determinant-fiber-trace-minimality} shows that
  \(\inf_{T\in\mathcal F(\mathcal D)}\|T\|_1=\tau_{\mathcal D}\). Determine
  the equality set
  \[
    \mathcal M(\mathcal D)
    :=
    \{T\in\mathcal F(\mathcal D):\|T\|_1=\tau_{\mathcal D}\}.
  \]
  Must every \(T\in\mathcal M(\mathcal D)\) be normal and unitarily
  equivalent to \(0_{H_0}\oplus L_{\mathcal D}\) for some \(H_0\), or can
  genuinely nonnormal minimizers occur?

  \item \textbf{Schatten-\(\mathcal S_p\) analogues.}
  For \(p>1\), let \(\det_p\) denote the regularized Fredholm determinant
  on \(\mathcal S_p\) in the sense of
  \cite[Chapter~9]{Simon2005TraceIdeals}. For one cyclic block,
  \[
    \begin{aligned}
      \det_p\!\bigl(I-z(C(n,\alpha)\otimes I_m)\bigr)
      &=(1-\beta z^n)^m \\
      &\quad {}\times
      \exp\!\left(
        m\sum_{1\le q\le (p-1)/n}\frac{\beta^q z^{qn}}{q}
      \right),
      \qquad \beta=\alpha^n.
    \end{aligned}
  \]
  Determine whether there is a sharp \(\mathcal S_p\)-summability
  condition on cyclic data that yields a normal block realization for a
  prescribed \(\det_p\)-germ, and whether the corresponding
  \(\mathcal S_p\)-fiber admits a canonical minimizer for the
  \(\|\cdot\|_p\)-norm.
\end{enumerate}
