\section{Deferred application directions}
\label{sec:perturbation}

The determinant-side viewpoint developed in \Cref{sec:fredholm} also
organizes several finite-dimensional application packages, but those
results are intentionally omitted from the present JST version. In
particular, the Abel finite-part formulas based on secondary Fredholm
factors, the twisted-determinant rigidity statements for locally constant
observables, and the spectral-radius decay and central-limit consequences
for twisted families all lie outside the core cyclic-block realization
theorem chain.

What is retained here is only the common input for those later projects:
the primitive-orbit identities from \Cref{sec:preliminaries}, the
Fredholm--Witt bridge, the cyclic-block realization theorem, and the
determinant rigidity mechanism. The purpose of this shortened section is
therefore only to mark that these finite-state determinant applications
can be developed separately without changing the scope of the present
manuscript.
