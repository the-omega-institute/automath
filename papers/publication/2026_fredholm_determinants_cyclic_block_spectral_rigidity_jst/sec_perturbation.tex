\section{Deferred sequel directions}
\label{sec:perturbation}

This paper stops with the cyclic-block realization theorem and spectral
rigidity. Beyond that boundary lie two sequel packages: Abel finite-part
formulas built from secondary Fredholm factors, and twisted determinant
results for locally constant observables, including spectral-radius
decay and limit-law consequences. Both require additional
finite-dimensional perturbative hypotheses and can be developed
separately from the theorem chain proved here.
